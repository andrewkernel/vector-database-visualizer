## Career Leverage Thesis

AI fluency is career leverage because it improves:

- Speed: faster drafts, research, prototypes, analysis
- Quality: better review, alternatives, critique
- Range: ability to touch coding, writing, design, automation, strategy
- Proof: visible projects and workflows
