# Source - WEF Future of Jobs 2025

Source: World Economic Forum, The Future of Jobs Report 2025.  
URL: https://www.weforum.org/publications/the-future-of-jobs-report-2025/  
Accessed: 2026-06-24

## Source Facts

- Published on 2025-01-07.
- The report examines labor-market transformation from 2025 to 2030.
- It is based on perspectives from over 1,000 global employers representing more than 14 million workers across 22 industry clusters and 55 economies.
- WEF identifies technological change, geoeconomic fragmentation, economic uncertainty, demographic shifts, and the green transition as major labor-market drivers.
