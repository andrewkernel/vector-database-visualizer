## Daily Energy Protocol

Morning:

- Get light early if possible.
- Drink water before heavy caffeine.
- Decide whether today is a push day, maintenance day, or recovery day.
- Choose work that fits energy instead of pretending every day is the same.

During work:

- Use 25 to 50 minute focus blocks.
- Keep phone friction high.
- Use AI to break tasks down when resistance appears.

Evening:

- Write tomorrow's first action in [[Daily Review]].
- Reduce open loops.
- Avoid starting high-stimulation work right before sleep.
