## Operating Thesis

This vault is not just a notebook. It is a context engine for your future AI work. The more clearly you keep goals, examples, preferences, project history, decisions, and workflows here, the better your AI conversations become.

Use the vault in three modes:

- Capture mode: throw raw thoughts into [[Inbox]].
- Thinking mode: connect notes to maps and ask better questions.
- Execution mode: open a workflow note, paste relevant context into AI, and move.

The goal is not to remember everything. The goal is to build a system that makes useful context available at the moment of action.
