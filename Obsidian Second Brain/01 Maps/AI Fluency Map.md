Daily practice loop:

1. Notice a task with friction.
2. Ask AI to help frame the work.
3. Provide context, constraints, examples, and desired output.
4. Critique the result.
5. Save the reusable workflow.

Questions to keep alive:

- What can AI help me see that I am missing?
- What context would make the answer 10x better?
- Where do I need verification instead of confidence?
- What workflow can I turn into a repeatable template?

## Fluency Levels

Level 1: User

- Can ask useful questions
- Can rewrite, summarize, brainstorm, and plan
- Knows when to verify

Level 2: Operator

- Builds repeatable prompts and checklists
- Uses AI across daily planning, coding, learning, and communication
- Maintains a [[Personal AI Stack]]

Level 3: Builder

- Uses APIs, tools, automations, agents, and structured outputs
- Can evaluate outputs with tests or rubrics
- Builds small systems that compound

Level 4: Strategist

- Knows when AI changes the shape of a goal
- Designs workflows around human judgment plus machine leverage
- Understands risk, privacy, cost, and reliability
