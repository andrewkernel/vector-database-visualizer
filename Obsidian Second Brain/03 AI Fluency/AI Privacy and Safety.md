# AI Privacy and Safety

The more AI-fluent you become, the more important it is to manage what context you share.

## Never Paste Without Thinking

Avoid pasting:

- Passwords
- API keys
- Full financial details
- Medical records
- Government IDs
- Private messages without a reason
- Proprietary code or documents you do not have permission to share

## Minimize Context

Give the model enough to help, but no more than necessary.

Examples:

- Replace names with roles.
- Remove account numbers.
- Summarize sensitive background instead of pasting raw text.
- Use local tools when data should stay local.

## Risk Questions

Before using AI:

- What data am I sending?
- Where is it going?
- What could go wrong if it is stored, leaked, or misused?
- Is this high-stakes advice?
- Do I need a human expert or official source?

## Personal Policy

Default: AI is allowed for drafting, learning, planning, and analysis.

Require verification for:

- Health
- Money
- Law
- Security
- Current facts
- Anything that affects another person
