# AI Research Workflow

Use this when facts matter.

Steps:

- Define the question.
- Ask what sources would be authoritative.
- Search or provide sources.
- Extract claims.
- Compare across sources.
- Separate facts, interpretations, and guesses.
- Save the answer and source links.

Verification prompt:

```text
Separate what is directly supported by the sources from what is inference. List uncertainties and what would change your answer.
```
