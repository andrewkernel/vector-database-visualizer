# AI Fluency North Star

North star: become the kind of person who can use AI naturally across learning, coding, planning, writing, research, automation, and decision-making.

Observable signs:

- I ask better questions.
- I give models better context.
- I verify important outputs.
- I build reusable workflows.
- I use AI daily without becoming dependent on it.
- I understand enough fundamentals to reason about limits.
