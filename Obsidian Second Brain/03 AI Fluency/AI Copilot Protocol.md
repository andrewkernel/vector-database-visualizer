# AI Copilot Protocol

Use this before any meaningful task.

1. Define the job.
2. Provide context.
3. State constraints.
4. Ask for options.
5. Choose an approach.
6. Ask AI to execute or draft.
7. Critique and iterate.
8. Save the reusable pattern.

Universal prompt:

```text
You are my thinking partner for [task]. Context: [paste relevant notes]. Goal: [desired outcome]. Constraints: [time, tools, format, quality bar]. First, ask up to three clarifying questions if needed. Then propose a concise plan and the first draft/action.
```
