# Source - UNESCO AI Competency Framework

Source: UNESCO, AI competency framework for students.  
URL: https://www.unesco.org/en/articles/ai-competency-framework-students  
Accessed: 2026-06-24

## Source Facts

- The publication aims to help educators integrate AI learning objectives into curricula.
- It was published on 2024-08-08 and last updated on 2026-01-16.
- UNESCO describes 12 competencies across four dimensions:
  - Human-centred mindset
  - Ethics of AI
  - AI techniques and applications
  - AI system design
- The progression levels are Understand, Apply, and Create.

## Personal Translation

This is a useful structure for my own AI fluency:

- Understand: know what AI can and cannot do.
- Apply: use AI responsibly in real work.
- Create: build workflows, tools, and systems with AI.
