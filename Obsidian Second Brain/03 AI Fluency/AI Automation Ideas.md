# AI Automation Ideas

Automation candidates:

- Summarize daily notes into weekly review
- Turn meeting notes into tasks
- Generate flashcards from learning notes
- Draft emails from bullet points
- Classify inbox captures
- Create project status updates
- Watch folders for new files and summarize them
- Convert rough ideas into project briefs

Automation filter:

- Repeated often?
- Clear input and output?
- Low risk if wrong?
- Easy to review?
- Saves real time or attention?
