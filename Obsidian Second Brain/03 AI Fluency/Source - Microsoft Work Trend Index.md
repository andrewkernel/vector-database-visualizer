# Source - Microsoft Work Trend Index

Source: Microsoft WorkLab, Work Trend Index.  
URL: https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/worklab/work-trend-index  
Accessed: 2026-06-24

## Source Facts

- Microsoft describes the Work Trend Index as research and data on trends reshaping work.
- The page lists annual reports from 2024 through 2026 about AI at work, frontier firms, agents, and human agency.
- The 2026 report is titled "Agents, human agency, and the opportunity for every organization."
- The 2025 report is titled "2025: The year the Frontier Firm is born."
- Microsoft states that its reports use anonymized, aggregated trends and do not use customer content such as email, chat, documents, or meetings to produce reports.
