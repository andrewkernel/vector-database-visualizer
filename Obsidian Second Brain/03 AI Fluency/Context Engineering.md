# Context Engineering

Context engineering is deciding what the AI needs to know to produce useful work.

High-value context:

- The current goal
- Relevant files or notes
- Definitions and assumptions
- Examples of desired output
- Constraints and non-goals
- Previous attempts
- Audience and tone
- Evaluation criteria

Context checklist:

- Did I include the actual source material?
- Did I say what "good" means?
- Did I mention what to avoid?
- Did I ask for citations or verification when facts matter?
- Did I keep private information out unless necessary?
