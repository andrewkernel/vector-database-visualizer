# Source - NIST AI RMF

Source: National Institute of Standards and Technology, AI Risk Management Framework.  
URL: https://www.nist.gov/itl/ai-risk-management-framework  
Accessed: 2026-06-24

## Practical Takeaways

- NIST frames AI risk around impacts to individuals, organizations, and society.
- The AI RMF is voluntary and focused on trustworthy AI.
- NIST released AI RMF 1.0 on 2023-01-26.
- NIST released a generative AI profile, NIST AI 600-1, on 2024-07-26.
- The AI RMF is being revised as of the accessed page.

## How I Use This Personally

I am not running a large AI organization, but the risk posture still matters:

- Know the task stakes.
- Know what data I send.
- Verify important outputs.
- Keep a human accountable for decisions.
