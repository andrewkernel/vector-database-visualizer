# 90 Day AI Fluency Curriculum

This is the deeper path after [[30 Day AI Fluency Plan]].

## Month 1: Fluent User

Goal: use AI every day with judgment.

Weeks:

- Week 1: [[Prompting as Thinking]]
- Week 2: [[Context Engineering]]
- Week 3: [[AI Research Workflow]]
- Week 4: [[AI Evaluation Checklist]]

Proof of work:

- 20 saved prompts
- 5 source-backed notes
- 3 workflows in [[AI Workflow Library]]

## Month 2: AI Operator

Goal: build repeatable workflows.

Weeks:

- Week 5: [[AI Coding Workflow]]
- Week 6: [[Day-to-Day AI Use Cases]]
- Week 7: [[AI Automation Ideas]]
- Week 8: [[Personal AI Stack]]

Proof of work:

- One small AI-assisted coding project
- One automated or semi-automated workflow
- One published explanation of what you learned

## Month 3: AI Builder and Strategist

Goal: understand tools, risk, and leverage.

Weeks:

- Week 9: APIs and structured outputs
- Week 10: Retrieval and knowledge bases
- Week 11: Agents and tool use
- Week 12: Evaluation, privacy, and safety

Proof of work:

- A personal AI workflow library
- A small app or automation
- A written personal AI operating manual
