## Practical Takeaways

- Give clear instructions and relevant context.
- Use examples when output style or structure matters.
- Break complex work into smaller tasks.
- Ask the model to reason, critique, or verify when quality matters.
- Use tools and structured outputs when plain text is not enough.

## My Interpretation

Prompt engineering is less about magic phrases and more about operational clarity: goal, context, constraints, examples, process, and output format.
