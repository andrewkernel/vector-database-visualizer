# Conversation Prep

Before a meaningful conversation:

- What is the purpose?
- What do I know about them?
- What should I ask?
- What can I offer?
- What follow-up might matter?

AI prompt:

```text
Help me prepare for a conversation with [person]. Context: [notes]. Give me thoughtful questions, likely topics, and a concise follow-up plan.
```

## Question Bank

Learning:

- What are you trying to get better at right now?
- What changed your mind recently?
- What resource has been unusually useful?

Career:

- What does good work look like in your world?
- What skill is becoming more valuable?
- What do beginners misunderstand?

Personal:

- What has been giving you energy lately?
- What are you looking forward to?
- What should I ask you about next time?
