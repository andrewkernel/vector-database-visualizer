# Productivity

Productivity means making the important thing easier to do.

Useful systems:

- [[Daily Dashboard]]
- [[Morning Startup]]
- [[Weekly Review]]
- [[Goal Stack]]
- [[AI Copilot Protocol]]

Avoid optimizing tasks that should be deleted.

## Productivity Stack

Capture:

- [[Inbox]]
- Quick notes
- Voice notes if useful

Clarify:

- [[Daily Dashboard]]
- [[Personal Dashboard Questions]]

Execute:

- [[Focus Protocol]]
- 25-minute sprints
- AI planning prompts

Review:

- [[Daily Review]]
- [[Weekly Review]]

Improve:

- Convert repeated friction into [[AI Workflow Library]]

## Anti-Productivity Traps

- Building systems instead of doing work
- Tracking too much
- Confusing research with progress
- Letting AI produce plans you never execute
- Keeping dead projects alive
