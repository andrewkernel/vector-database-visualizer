# Focus Protocol

Use this when you are scattered.

## Reset

- Open [[Daily Dashboard]]
- Write the exact task
- Write why it matters
- Set a 25-minute timer
- Put one AI prompt or one next action in front of you

## Anti-Drift Questions

- What am I actually trying to finish?
- What is the next visible output?
- What would make this easier?
- Am I researching because I need to, or because I am avoiding execution?

## AI Focus Prompt

```text
I am distracted and need to make progress on [task]. Help me define a 25-minute sprint with one clear output, a starting step, and a stop condition.
```
