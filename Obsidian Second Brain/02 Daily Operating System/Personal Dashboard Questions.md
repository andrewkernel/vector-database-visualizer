# Personal Dashboard Questions

These questions keep the brain alive.

## Daily

- What deserves my best attention today?
- What can AI help me clarify or accelerate?
- What would make today feel successful?
- What am I avoiding?
- What is the next physical action?

## Weekly

- What repeated friction did I notice?
- Which goal got real evidence of progress?
- What did I learn that should become a note?
- What did I save into [[AI Workflow Library]]?
- Who should I reconnect with?

## Monthly

- What am I becoming better at?
- What still feels chaotic?
- What should I stop pretending matters?
- What opportunity is emerging?
